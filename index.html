About Us

A Century of Alpine Excellence

Welcome to ZettySki, where the love for winter sports meets a legacy that spans a century! Established in 1923 by the visionary adventurer Erastus Bingham, ZettySki has been at the forefront of providing unparalleled skiing experiences for enthusiasts around the world.

Our Founder's Unforgettable Journey: Erastus Bingham

In the early 1920s, the adventurous spirit of Erastus Bingham led him to the pristine slopes of the Swiss Alps. Drawn by the allure of the majestic peaks and the thrill of gliding through powdery snow, Erastus knew he had discovered a passion that would change his life and the lives of countless others.

Erastus Bingham, with a heart full of determination and a mind brimming with innovative ideas, founded ZettySki on the principles of quality, safety, and an unwavering commitment to the joy of skiing. His dream was to share the magic of winter sports with the world, and thus, the legacy of ZettySki was born.

ZettySki Today

Fast forward to the present day, and ZettySki stands as a symbol of excellence in the skiing community. Our commitment to Erastus Bingham's vision remains unwavering. We continue to provide top-notch ski equipment, expert guidance, and a passion for winter adventures that transcends generations.

At ZettySki, we take pride in being more than just a ski rental company; we are a community of winter enthusiasts united by the thrill of the slopes. Our 100 years of experience have allowed us to curate a selection of the finest skis and snowboarding gear, ensuring that each moment on the snow is nothing short of extraordinary.

Join the ZettySki Legacy

As we celebrate our centennial year, we invite you to join us in the next chapter of the ZettySki legacy. Whether you are a seasoned pro or a first-time adventurer, ZettySki is your gateway to the ultimate alpine experience.

Here's to the next hundred years of carving memories, forging friendships, and embracing the sheer joy of the slopes. Welcome to ZettySki - where the adventure never ends!
